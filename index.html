1Jeu1Ko

Welcome to the 1Jeu1Ko project, where games have to weigth less than 1Ko. Here are the games currently available:

  • Flappy Bird;
  • Falling Stars;
  • Golf;
  • Tic Tac Toe.

Some games unfortunately did not meet the 1Ko limit, usually not by much. You can still try them out here:

  • RPG;
  • A maze.

These games are minimalistic, and no instruction will be given on how to play: try them out, click everywhere, or read their source code. Enjoy!
